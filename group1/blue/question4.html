..with a soft voice, the ghost asks:

Ghost: Last question..

Ghost: How did you die?


A rush of emotions come through you - saddness, happiness, love, hate!

It is almost too much for you to handle, but you cannot back down now.


You see a room, the same room you are in right now.

It is your bedroom.

Your first love is now your wife and she is sleeping right next to you.

You are at peace. Up to that moment you have lived a good life and nothing can go wrong.

You blink.

The room is on fire.

You try waking up your wife

She is not responding - you are scared to death.

The windows won't open, the door is blocked

The fire is engulfing everything.

You hear sirens and firefighters coming up the stairs

There is hope!

With your last strenght, you manage to squeeze your wife through the door..

..but it is too late for you.
