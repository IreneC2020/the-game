Ghost: You see, at times I get so lonely that I want a companion.

You: So you brought me here?

Ghost: You are a silly one. I sometimes think of silly things too.

Ghost: How can I bring you here when I can't even leave this room.

You: So how did I get in here?

Ghost: Remember all those stories you told me about?

You: I didn't tell you a word...

Ghost: Yes.. but I knew all those things, but I like to remind myself of them sometimes.

You: How do you know so much about my life?

Ghost: Your life?! I was there.

You: I don't understand...

Ghost: You see, you and I are one and the same.

Ghost: I've created you, just like I've created a million more of you before.

Ghost: You are just a part of my imagination that helps me cope with this loneliness.

Ghost: By morning you will be gone and I will have to invent another one of you.
